# Key Insights from Student Performance Dashboard

## Academic Performance
- Computer Networks has the highest average marks among all subjects.
- Mathematics shows a wider variation in student scores compared to other subjects.

## Department-wise Analysis
- CSE students demonstrate higher average attendance compared to IT students.
- Higher attendance generally correlates with better academic performance.

## Semester-wise Trends
- Semester 4 students show slightly higher average marks than Semester 3.
- Performance consistency improves in higher semesters.

## Overall Observation
The dashboard highlights the importance of attendance and subject-wise trends in evaluating student performance. Interactive filters help compare departments and semesters effectively.
